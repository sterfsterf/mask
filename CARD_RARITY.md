# Card Rarity System

## Rarities

### Common (70% drop rate)
Basic, reliable cards with straightforward effects.
- Slash (1 energy, 5 dmg)
- Defend (1 energy, 5 block)
- Heavy Strike (2 energy, 12 dmg)
- Rage Strike (1 energy, 9 dmg, 2 self dmg)
- Iron Defense (2 energy, 12 block)
- Feral Bite (1 energy, 8 dmg)
- Quick Strike (0 energy, 3 dmg)
- Burrow (1 energy, 8 block)
- Plague Touch (1 energy, 5 dmg, 1 poison)
- Rage Strike Trait (1 energy, 9 dmg)
- Fortify (1 energy, 8 block)
- Swift Strike (1 energy, 4 dmg)
- Mend Mask (2 energy, heal 8 mask HP)

### Rare (25% drop rate)
Powerful cards with strong effects or combos.
- Void Strike (2 energy, 14 dmg)
- Backstab (1 energy, 10 dmg)
- Crushing Blow (2 energy, 16 dmg, 4 self dmg)
- Venom Strike (1 energy, 4 dmg, 2 poison)
- Adrenaline (0 energy, +2 energy next turn)
- Empower Mask (1 energy, mask cards +2)
- Empower Soul (1 energy, soul cards +2)
- Stunning Blow (2 energy, 8 dmg, stun)
- Poisoned Defense (1 energy, 6 block, 1 poison)
- Mask Sacrifice (1 energy, convert 6 mask HP to soul HP)

### Legendary (5% drop rate)
Game-changing one-time use cards or extreme power.
- Toxic Cloud (2 energy, 5 poison, VOID)
- Embrace Scars (2 energy, scar cards +3, VOID)
- Concussive Strike (1 energy, 3 dmg, stun, VOID)
- Soul Restoration (3 energy, heal 12 soul HP, VOID)
- Desperate Strike (0 energy, 18 dmg, 6 self dmg, VOID)
- Corrupted Power (2 energy, soul cards +3, 2 poison self)

### Curse (defeat penalty)
Debuff cards added to deck on defeat.
- Tired (0 energy, unplayable)
- Cracked (0 energy, unplayable)
- Hesitation (2 energy, 3 dmg - overcosted)
- Drain (0 energy, unplayable)
- Exhaustion (1 energy, -1 energy next turn)

## Victory Rewards

After winning a battle, choose 1 of 3 randomly generated cards:
- 70% chance for common
- 25% chance for rare
- 5% chance for legendary

Cards are added to your mask (if equipped) or forfeited.

## Defeat Penalties

After losing a battle, your mask breaks and you must choose 1 of 3 curse cards to add to your soul's deck permanently (until soul dies).

## Rarity Colors
- Common: Gray (#888)
- Rare: Blue (#4a9eff)
- Legendary: Orange (#ff9d00)
- Curse: Purple (#8b00ff)
